Creative Concept March 05, 2026

Refracting Reality: The
Prism & Shadow Experiment

Break the boundaries of traditional portraiture. Discover how we use optical glass, prisms, and hard-light shadows to create surreal, multi-layered compositions right on the studio floor.

Concept: Optical Distortion
Studio: Eclipse Suite
The Concept

The "Fractured"
Aesthetic

This concept relies on the physical manipulation of light before it reaches the lens. By placing high-grade triangular prisms and convex glass spheres directly in front of the camera, we create light leaks and rainbow flares that feel organic rather than digital.

Hard Key Light

Creates sharp, defined shadows that contrast with soft light leaks.

Negative Space

Allows the refracted light to "breathe" against a dark canvas.

Execution

Building the Set

📐

Angle of Incidence

The magic happens at the 45-degree mark. Position your prism just at the edge of the lens to catch the stray rays from our Profoto D2s.

🔍

Manual Focus Lock

When shooting through glass, your AF might struggle. We recommend using the "Focus Peaking" on our Sony A7R V to lock onto the eyes through the distortion.

✨

Post-Pro Sync

Because these effects are captured in-camera, our on-site digital techs can help you enhance the saturation of the flares during the live tethering session.
